Why does the level keep killing me?

Because it's lying. Floors resign mid-step, doors run away, the level-clear screen itself is occasionally a trap. Every one of the 50 levels hides ONE signature betrayal — your job is to stop trusting and start jumping anyway.

What is the Daily Troll?

One new level a day, the same for everyone on Earth. Build a streak, compare deaths, and watch the ghosts of everyone who fell for it before you — their last runs replay faintly as you play.

Do the ads ruin it?

No — that's the whole point. Sus Levels uses respectful ads: die a lot on a level and we leave you alone (frustration grace), never mid-level, never forced difficulty. Remove them once for $4.99 if you like, or take the Imp Bribe.

Is it actually beatable?

Every level — including mirrored remix mode — is machine-verified beatable before it ships. Speedrun leaderboards only accept runs whose input recordings replay successfully. No honor system. The imp checks.
