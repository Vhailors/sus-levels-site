Sus Levels — Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-06-13

What we collect

Sus Levels stores your progress, settings and purchases locally on your device. If the optional online features are enabled, the game sends anonymous gameplay events (level completed, death counts) and anonymous death-replay coordinates (position traces only — no personal data, no identifiers) to our servers to power global counters, daily levels and ghost replays. These cannot be linked to you. Gameplay analytics are processed by PostHog and purchase totals by RevenueCat under a random install identifier that is not connected to your name, email or any account.

Ads & purchases

Ad mediation partners (when ads are enabled) may process device identifiers under their own policies, used solely for ad delivery and frequency capping. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play / Apple; we never see payment details.

What we don't do

No accounts. No emails. No contact lists. No location. No selling data. No tracking across other apps or sites.

Children

The game is suitable for general audiences and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all local data. Anonymous server events contain nothing attributable to delete.
